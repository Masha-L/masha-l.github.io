Once a month, we press pause. We gather around a table — not for anything grand, just for the kind of morning that feels like it could stretch on forever. Good food, easy conversation, the people who make the city feel smaller and warmer.

The Last Saturday is an open invitation to show up exactly as you are. Bring something to share — a dish, a story, a bottle of something sparkling. Leave whenever feels right.
