Privacy Policy

Bubble Stack: Drop & Merge Puzzle

Last Updated: February 18, 2026
Effective Date: February 18, 2026

Introduction

Lovelace Games ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use Bubble Stack (the "Game").

TL;DR: Bubble Stack is an offline puzzle game. We don't collect personal data, don't require accounts, and don't track you. Crash reports may be collected automatically by the app store platform to help us fix bugs.

What Information We Collect

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • No personal information — No name, email, phone number, or address
  • No account registration — Play without signing up
  • No location data — We don't access your GPS or location
  • No contacts or photos — We don't access your device files
  • No advertising identifiers — We don't track you across apps
  • No behavioral tracking — We don't monitor how you play

Information That May Be Collected Automatically

Crash Reports & Analytics (via Platform)

When the Game crashes, your device's operating system (iOS or Android) may automatically collect and send crash reports to us through Apple App Store Connect or Google Play Console. This data typically includes:

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version
  • App version
  • Crash logs and error information
  • General device settings (language, region)

This information is anonymous and aggregated — it does not identify you personally. We use it solely to identify and fix bugs.

Note: This crash reporting is handled by Apple and Google's standard developer tools, not by any third-party analytics service we've added.

Local Data Storage

Your game progress (high scores, preferences, theme selection) is stored locally on your device only. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not transmitted to any server
  • Is not backed up to our systems
  • Can be deleted by uninstalling the Game

How We Use Information

We use crash report data only to:

  • Fix bugs and improve Game stability
  • Ensure compatibility with different devices
  • Improve the Game experience

We do not use any information for:

  • Advertising or marketing
  • Selling to third parties
  • Building user profiles
  • Any purpose unrelated to Game improvement

Third-Party Services

Current Status: Bubble Stack does not include any third-party SDKs, analytics services, or advertising networks.

Future Updates: If we add third-party services (such as ads) in future versions, this Privacy Policy will be updated, and you will be notified through an app update.

Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Bubble Stack is designed to be safe for players of all ages.

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or under 16 in certain jurisdictions)
  • We do not require any personal information to play the Game
  • The Game does not contain social features, chat, or user-generated content
  • The Game does not contain advertisements

Parents: If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us immediately at the email below, and we will delete it.

GDPR Compliance (European Users)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or Switzerland:

  • Legal Basis: Any processing of crash report data is based on our legitimate interest in maintaining a functional, bug-free Game
  • Your Rights: You have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict, or object to processing of your data. However, since we don't collect identifiable personal data, these rights are limited in practice
  • Data Controller: Lovelace Games is the data controller

Since we do not collect personal data, there is generally no personal data to access, correct, or delete.

Data Retention

  • Crash reports: Retained by Apple/Google according to their standard policies (typically 90 days to 2 years)
  • Local game data: Stored on your device until you uninstall the Game

Data Security

We maintain reasonable security practices. Since we don't collect personal data or operate servers that store user information, the primary security responsibility lies with Apple and Google's platform infrastructure.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do:

  • The "Last Updated" date at the top will change
  • Material changes may be communicated via app update notes
  • Continued use of the Game after changes constitutes acceptance

Your Choices

  • Crash Reports: You can disable crash reporting in your device settings (iOS: Settings → Privacy → Analytics; Android: Settings → Google → Usage & Diagnostics)
  • Local Data: Delete all game data by uninstalling the Game
